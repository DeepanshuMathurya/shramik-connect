How ROZGAAR Bridges the Ground Reality

Core Pitch & Technical Innovations

01

The Technology Barrier

Problem: Blue-collar workers often struggle with complex English text interfaces or email registrations.
Our Solution: A minimal text, heavily visual dashboard architecture designed for rapid, mobile-first comprehension.

02

Accidental Ghosting (IVR Call Lock)

Problem: Workers might tap buttons accidentally, causing contractors to waste time on no-shows.
Our Solution: Automated double-confirmation architecture simulating real-time IVR voice phone validations.

03

Priority Rating Sorting

Problem: Contractors need dependable, high-quality execution without digging through hundreds of names.
Our Solution: An automated sorting algorithm that pushes the highest-rated reliable workers directly to the top.
